Pricing questions, answered

Is there a free trial?

No — Mac App Store doesn't support trials for paid apps. Apple's 14-day refund policy gives you the same window in practice.

What about subscriptions?

None. SnapDog is one-time. We don't sell add-ons, lockouts, or premium tiers. The feature you saw on the website is the feature you'll get.

Updates after launch?

1.x updates are free for life. A future 2.x major version may carry a paid upgrade (typical for indie Mac apps); 1.x will continue to receive bug fixes either way.

Are uploads to Imgur / S3 / etc. free?

SnapDog itself is. The destinations you upload to (Imgur, S3, Dropbox, Cloudinary, etc.) bill you directly per their own terms — we don't proxy traffic or take a cut.

Does the AI cost extra?

No. The Built-In Demo runs on-device with no account, key, or network. If you choose an optional cloud provider, any provider charges are billed by that provider directly.

Volume / EDU discounts?

Mac App Store doesn't support either. Sorry.
