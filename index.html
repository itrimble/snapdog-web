Screenshot · Record · Annotate

Your whole capture workflow, one menu bar icon.

SnapDog is a fast, native macOS menu-bar app for capturing, annotating, and sharing your screen — screen recording with webcam picture-in-picture, a full annotation editor, OCR, QR and color tools, and one-tap upload to 19 destinations. Without launching another app.

Everything a capture app should do. Nothing it shouldn't.

Capture anything

Fullscreen, window, or pixel-precise region with a 4× magnifier loupe and live coordinate readout. Delayed captures, plus user-driven stitch capture for pages taller than your screen.

Record like a studio

Screen recording in MP4, MOV, or GIF with a draggable, resizable webcam picture-in-picture, webcam-only mode, pause / resume, click-ripple visualizer, and system + microphone audio.

A real annotation editor

Arrows, shapes, text, speech balloons, step counters, spotlight, highlighter, blur and pixelate — plus crop, straighten, a layers-style objects panel, undo / redo, and PNG / JPEG / TIFF / HEIC export.

Tools you'll actually use

Recognize on-screen text with OCR, decode and generate QR codes, sample any pixel with the color picker's magnifier loupe, and measure distances with the on-screen ruler.

On-device transcription

Recordings are transcribed with Apple's on-device speech engine — the audio never leaves your Mac. Auto-transcribe every recording, then summarize the transcript with your own AI provider.

Automate the boring part

Hotkey-triggered capture workflows, post-capture Actions, 19 upload destinations from Imgur to S3 to SFTP, webhooks with HMAC-SHA256 signing, and drag-in ShareX .sxcu uploader support.

Capture → Annotate → Share

Three steps, zero app-switching.

1 · Capture

Hit a hotkey — or let SnapDog take over the system ⇧⌘3 / ⇧⌘4 / ⇧⌘5 shortcuts — and grab a screen, window, region, or recording.

2 · Annotate

The capture opens straight in SnapDog's editor: arrows, callouts, blur, step counters, watermarks, and one-click Beautify frames.

3 · Share or automate

Copy, save, or pin it to your screen — or let a workflow upload it and post the link to Slack, Discord, or a webhook automatically.

Private by default

SnapDog collects nothing. Your captures are yours.

No analytics, no telemetry

No usage analytics, no crash reports sent to external servers, no advertising identifiers or tracking. Ever.

No account required

There's nothing to sign up for. Buy it once on the Mac App Store and it's yours — no login, no cloud dependency.

Captures stay on your Mac

Nothing is uploaded unless you opt in to a destination. AI image analysis includes a built-in on-device demo; optional provider requests go directly from your Mac to the provider you choose.

Secrets live in the Keychain

API keys, OAuth tokens, SFTP passwords and private keys, and webhook signing secrets are stored in the macOS Keychain — never in plain files.
